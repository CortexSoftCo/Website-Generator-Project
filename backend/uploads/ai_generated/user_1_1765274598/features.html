Powerful Features to Elevate Your Studio

Everything you need, nothing you don't. Designed for simplicity and growth.

Intuitive Class Scheduling

Build your schedule in minutes. Create recurring classes, one-off workshops, or private appointments. Our clean, visual calendar makes it easy to see your week at a glance and for students to book their spot.

Online Booking & Waitlists

Let your students book and pay from any device, anytime. Our system automatically manages class capacity and waitlists, sending notifications when a spot opens up. Say goodbye to back-and-forth emails.

Secure Payment Processing

Integrate with Stripe to accept all major credit cards. Sell single classes, class packs, or recurring memberships. All transactions are secure, and payouts go directly to your bank account.

Reporting & Analytics

Gain valuable insights into your business. Track attendance, revenue, and popular classes. Make data-driven decisions to grow your studio and better serve your community.
