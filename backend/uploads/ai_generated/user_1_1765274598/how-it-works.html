Get Started in 3 Simple Steps

From sign-up to your first booking in just a few minutes.

Sign Up & Set Up Your Studio

Create your ZenFlow account in under a minute. We'll guide you through adding your studio's name, logo, and location. Define your class types, from Vinyasa Flow to Restorative Yin.
