Frequently Asked Pricing Questions

Can I change my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan at any time from your account settings.

Is there a free trial?

Absolutely! All plans come with a full-featured 14-day free trial. No credit card is required to get started.

What are the transaction fees?

ZenFlow does not charge any extra transaction fees. You only pay the standard processing fees from our payment partner, Stripe.
