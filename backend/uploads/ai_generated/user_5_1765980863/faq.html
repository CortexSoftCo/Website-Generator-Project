Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our courses, platform, and policies.

General Questions

Who are the courses for?

Our courses are designed for a wide range of individuals, from complete beginners looking to switch careers to experienced tech professionals aiming to upskill in specialized areas like MLOps or advanced cloud architecture.

What makes TechElevate different from other platforms?

We focus on three key pillars: expert instructors who are active industry practitioners, a project-based curriculum that ensures you build real-world skills, and dedicated career support to help you achieve your goals.

Courses & Learning

Are there any prerequisites for the beginner courses?

No! Our beginner-level courses are designed for individuals with no prior experience in the field. All you need is a computer, an internet connection, and a willingness to learn.

Do I get a certificate after completing a course?

Yes, upon successful completion of any course, you will receive a verifiable certificate that you can share on your LinkedIn profile and resume to showcase your new skills.

How long do I have access to the course materials?

For single course purchases, you have lifetime access to all course materials, including videos and resources. For subscriptions, you have access as long as your subscription is active.

Payments & Subscriptions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) through our secure payment processor, Stripe. We also support payments via PayPal.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied for any reason, contact our support team within 30 days of purchase for a full refund.

Still have questions?

Our support team is ready to help. Get in touch with us today.
