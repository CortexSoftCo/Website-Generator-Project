Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We have answers. Find the information you're looking for below.

What kind of businesses do you work with?

We work with a wide range of small to medium-sized businesses across various industries, including e-commerce, healthcare, professional services, real estate, and more. If you have repetitive digital processes, we can likely help you automate them.

How long does a typical automation project take?

The timeline varies depending on the complexity of the project. A simple, single-process automation (like our Starter package) can often be completed within 1-2 weeks. More complex, multi-system integrations may take 4-8 weeks. We provide a detailed project timeline after our initial discovery call.

I'm not technical. Will I be able to understand the process?

Absolutely. Our entire process is designed for non-technical business owners. We speak in plain English, focus on your business goals, and handle all the technical implementation. We provide clear documentation and training so you feel confident using your new automated systems.

What is the expected return on investment (ROI)?

The ROI comes in many forms: direct cost savings from reduced labor hours, increased revenue from faster sales cycles, improved accuracy by eliminating human error, and enhanced customer satisfaction. During our consultation, we'll work with you to estimate the potential ROI for your specific project.

Do you provide support after the project is complete?

Yes. All of our projects come with a 30-day post-launch support period to ensure everything is running smoothly. We also offer ongoing support and maintenance retainers for businesses that want continuous optimization and peace of mind.
